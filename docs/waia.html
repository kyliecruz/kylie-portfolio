Waterloo AI Association (WAIA)

WAIA is a student-led community in the Waterloo region focused on AI safety, governance, and responsible AI. We host discussions, speaker events, reading groups, and resources for students who want to build real skills and contribute thoughtfully.

What we do
  • Intro sessions + reading groups (AI safety + governance)
  • Guest speakers + community events
  • Opportunities + resources for students
